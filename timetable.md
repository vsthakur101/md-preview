# Weekly Timetable: Work + Gym + Study

## Weekdays (Monday-Friday)

### Morning Routine (6:00 AM - 9:30 AM)
- **6:00 AM - 6:15 AM**: Wake up & freshen up
- **6:15 AM - 6:30 AM**: Light breakfast & coffee
- **6:30 AM - 7:30 AM**: **Gym Session** (Strength/Cardio)
- **7:30 AM - 8:00 AM**: Post-workout shower & recovery
- **8:00 AM - 8:30 AM**: Breakfast + prepare for work
- **8:30 AM - 9:00 AM**: Commute to work
- **9:00 AM - 9:30 AM**: Review study goals/day planning

### Work Hours (9:30 AM - 6:30 PM)
- **9:30 AM - 6:00 PM**: **Work** (includes lunch break)
- **6:00 PM - 6:30 PM**: Commute home

### Evening Routine (6:30 PM - 10:30 PM)
- **6:30 PM - 7:15 PM**: Dinner & family/personal time
- **7:15 PM - 7:30 PM**: Quick mental break
- **7:30 PM - 9:00 PM**: **Focused Study Session 1** 
- **9:00 PM - 9:15 PM**: Short break
- **9:15 PM - 10:15 PM**: **Focused Study Session 2**
- **10:15 PM - 10:30 PM**: Wind down, review tomorrow's goals
- **10:30 PM**: Bedtime

## Saturday

### Morning
- **7:00 AM - 8:30 AM**: **Gym Session** (Longer workout)
- **8:30 AM - 9:00 AM**: Breakfast & shower
- **9:00 AM - 12:00 PM**: **Study Block 1** (Deep work)

### Afternoon
- **12:00 PM - 2:00 PM**: Lunch & personal time
- **2:00 PM - 5:00 PM**: **Study Block 2** (Practice/assignments)

### Evening
- **5:00 PM onwards**: Personal time, hobbies, social activities

## Sunday

### Morning
- **8:00 AM - 9:00 AM**: Light exercise or yoga
- **9:00 AM - 11:00 AM**: **Weekly Planning & Review**
- **11:00 AM - 1:00 PM**: **Study Block** (Revision/catch-up)

### Afternoon
- **1:00 PM - 3:00 PM**: Lunch & rest
- **3:00 PM - 5:00 PM**: Meal prep for week

### Evening
- **5:00 PM onwards**: Complete rest, family time, entertainment

## Daily Study Tips

### Weekday Study (3 hours total)
- **Session 1 (7:30-9:00 PM)**: Theory/new concepts
- **Session 2 (9:15-10:15 PM)**: Practice/applications

### Weekend Study (5-6 hours total)
- Focus on difficult topics
- Work on assignments/projects
- Review previous week's material

## Time Management Strategies

### 1. Time Blocking
- Use calendar to block study/gym time
- Treat these blocks as non-negotiable

### 2. Energy Management
- Morning gym for energy boost
- Study when mentally fresh (evening after rest)

### 3. Productivity Hacks
- Pomodoro technique during study (25 min study, 5 min break)
- Pre-prep meals to save time
- Use commute time for audio learning

### 4. Weekly Planning
- Sunday planning session for upcoming week
- Set realistic daily goals
- Track progress and adjust as needed

## Flexibility Guidelines

### If you miss gym:
- Do 15-minute home workout
- Reschedule to next day with lighter workout

### If too tired to study:
- Review notes instead of new material
- Watch educational videos instead of active study
- Use study apps for passive learning

### If work extends late:
- Shorten study sessions but don't skip entirely
- Prioritize most important study tasks
